What is One Piece?

One Piece (Japanese: ワ ン ピ ー ス Hepburn: Wan Pīsu) is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Eiichiro Oda. This manga has been published in Shueisha's Weekly Shonen Jump magazine since July 22, 1997, and has been bundled into 91 tankōbon volumes. The story tells the adventure of Monkey D. Luffy, a boy who has elastic ability like rubber after getting fruit. With his pirate crew, called the Straw Hat Pirates, Luffy searched for the Grand Line to find the world's biggest treasure known as "One Piece" in order to become the next Pirate King. This manga has been adapted into an original animation video (OVA) sent by Production IG in 1998, and an anime series presented by Toei Animation, which began airing in Japan in 1999.
